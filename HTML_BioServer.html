About Influenza Bioserver

We introduce our Influenza Bioserver, which is mainly concerned with DNA Sequences.
We are interested in Influenza Virus A; as our main biological sequence.
There are many biological functions that can be performed on sequences, whether those sequences are in our database or you want to enter them yourself.

About Biosequences Analysis

Biological sequence analysis compares, aligns, indexes, and analyzes biological sequences
and thus plays a crucial role in bioinformatics and modern biology.
Sequence alignment is based on the fact that all living organisms are related by evolution.

About Influenza Virus A

Influenza A is a type of virus that causes influenza (the flu) a highly contagious respiratory illness.
The other types of influenza virus are type B and type C.
If you get it, you will need to rest at home and avoid infecting others.
Vaccination can protect you against influenza A.

Some Of Available Biological Functions

Calculate GC Content

The percentage of G + C, is widely used to measure genomic nucleotide composition.
It is calculated as
Count (G + C) / Count (A + T + G + C) * 100%.

DNA Transcription

Transcription is the process by which the information in a strand of DNA is copied into a new molecule of messenger RNA (mRNA).

DNA Complement
&
Reverse Complement

Reverse Complement converts a DNA sequence into its reverse, complement, or reverse-complement counterpart.
You may want to work with the reverse-complement of a sequence if it contains an ORF on the reverse strand.

DNA Translation

The process through which information encoded in messenger RNA (mRNA) directs the addition of amino acids during protein synthesis.
